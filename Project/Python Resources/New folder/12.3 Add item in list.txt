<h1><span style="color: #000080;">Add items to list:</span></h1>
<p>Python provides <span style="color: #800000;">append()</span> method to add an item to list.</p>
<p><strong>Note:</strong></p>
<p><span style="color: #800000;">append()</span> method will add the new item to the end of the list.</p>
<p><strong>Fore example:</strong></p>

<tbody>

<h2><span style="color: #000080;">Extend list:</span></h2>
<p>Python allows the programmer to append items from another list to the existing list.</p>
<p><strong>For example:</strong></p>
<table style="margin: auto; box-shadow: 3px 3px 10px #000;" border="1">
<tbody>
<tr style="background-color: rgba(0,0,0,0.1);">
<td>
<p>list1 = [<span style="color: #008000;">"Benz"</span>, <span style="color: #008000;">"BMW"</span>, <span style="color: #008000;">"Toyota"</span>]</p>
<p>list2 = [<span style="color: #008000;">"Ford"</span>, <span style="color: #008000;">"Honda"</span>, <span style="color: #008000;">"Hyundai"</span>]</p>
<p>list1.<span style="color: #800000;">extend</span>(list2)</p>
<p><span style="color: #800000;">print</span>(list1)</p>
</td>
</tr>
</tbody>
</table>
<p><strong>Note:</strong></p>
<p><span style="color: #800000;">extend()</span> method will work the same for any other type of inerrable object.</p>
