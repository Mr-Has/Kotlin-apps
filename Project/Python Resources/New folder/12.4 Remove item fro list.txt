<h2><span style="color: #000080;">Delete by index:</span></h2>
<p><span style="color: #800000;">pop()</span> method enables programmer to delete an item with a specific index.</p>
<p><strong>For example:</strong></p>
<table style="margin: auto; box-shadow: 3px 3px 10px #000;" border="1">
<tbody>
<tr style="background-color: rgba(0,0,0,0.1);">
<td>thelist = [<span style="color: #008000;">"Benz"</span>, <span style="color: #008000;">"BMW"</span>, <span style="color: #008000;">"Toyota"</span>]<br />thelist.<span style="color: #800000;">pop</span>(<span style="color: #800000;">1</span>)<br /><span style="color: #800000;">print</span>(thelist)</td>
</tr>
</tbody>
</table>

<h2><span style="color: #000080;">Keyword del:</span></h2>

<p><strong>For example:</strong></p>
<table style="margin: auto; box-shadow: 3px 3px 10px #000;" border="1">
<tbody>
<tr style="background-color: rgba(0,0,0,0.1);">

</tbody>
</table>
