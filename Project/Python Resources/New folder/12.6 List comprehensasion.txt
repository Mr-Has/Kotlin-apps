<h2><span style="color: #000080;">List comprehension:</span></h2>
<p>List comprehension provide a shorter syntax for creating a new list from existing list with condition.</p>
<p><strong>For example:</strong></p>
<table style="margin: auto; box-shadow: 3px 3px 10px #000;" border="1">
<tbody>
<tr style="background-color: rgba(0,0,0,0.1);">
<td>
<p>cars = [<span style="color: #008000;">"Benz"</span>, <span style="color: #008000;">"BMW"</span>, <span style="color: #008000;">"Toyota"</span>, <span style="color: #008000;">"Honda"</span>, <span style="color: #008000;">"Ford"</span>]</p>
<p>newlist = [i <span style="color: #800000;">for</span> i <span style="color: #800000;">in</span> cars <span style="color: #800000;">if</span> "a" <span style="color: #800000;">in</span> i]</p>
<p><span style="color: #800000;">print</span>(newlist)</p>
</td>
</tr>
</tbody>
</table>

<table style="margin: auto; box-shadow: 3px 3px 10px #000;" border="1">
<tbody>
<tr style="background-color: rgba(0,0,0,0.1);">
<td>
