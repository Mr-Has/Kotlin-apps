<h2><span style="color: #000080;">Range indexing:</span></h2>
<p>Python allows us to access a range of items in a tuple:</p>
<p><strong>For example:</strong></p>
<table style="margin: auto; box-shadow: 3px 3px 10px #000;" border="1">
<tbody>
<tr style="background-color: rgba(0,0,0,0.1);">
<td>
<p>thetuple = (<span style="color: #008000;">"Benz"</span>,<span style="color: #008000;">"BMW"</span>,<span style="color: #008000;">"Toyota"</span>,<span style="color: #008000;">"Honda"</span>,</p>
<p>&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; <span style="color: #008000;">&nbsp;"Ford"</span>,<span style="color: #008000;">"GMC"</span>,<span style="color: #008000;">"Fiat"</span>)<br /><span style="color: #800000;">print</span>(thetuple[<span style="color: #800000;">3</span>:<span style="color: #800000;">6</span>])</p>
<p><span style="color: #808080;"># Returns:&nbsp;('Honda', 'Ford', 'GMC')</span></p>
</td>
</tr>
</tbody>
</table>
<p><strong>Note:</strong></p>
<p>Python will start from index 0 of the tuple if we leave the starting point empty.</p>
<p><strong>For example:</strong></p>
<table style="margin: auto; box-shadow: 3px 3px 10px #000;" border="1">
<tbody>
<tr style="background-color: rgba(0,0,0,0.1);">
<td>
<p>thetuple = (<span style="color: #008000;">"Benz"</span>,<span style="color: #008000;">"BMW"</span>,<span style="color: #008000;">"Toyota"</span>,<span style="color: #008000;">"Honda"</span>,<br />&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;<span style="color: #008000;">"Ford"</span>,<span style="color: #008000;">"GMC"<span style="color: #000000;">,</span></span><span style="color: #008000;">"Fiat"</span>)<br /><span style="color: #800000;">print</span>(thetuple[:<span style="color: #800000;">3</span>])</p>
<p><span style="color: #808080;"># Returns: ('Benz', 'BMW', 'Toyota')</span></p>
</td>
</tr>
</tbody>
</table>
<p><strong>Note:</strong></p>
<p>Python will continue to the end of tuple if we leave the ending point empty.</p>
<p><strong>For example:</strong></p>
<table style="margin: auto; box-shadow: 3px 3px 10px #000;" border="1">
<tbody>
<tr style="background-color: rgba(0,0,0,0.1);">
<td>
<p>thetuple = (<span style="color: #008000;">"Benz"</span>,<span style="color: #008000;">"BMW"</span>,<span style="color: #008000;">"Toyota"</span>,<span style="color: #008000;">"Honda"</span>,<br />&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;<span style="color: #008000;">"Ford"</span>,<span style="color: #008000;">"GMC"</span>,<span style="color: #008000;">"Fiat"</span>)<br /><span style="color: #800000;">print</span>(thetuple[<span style="color: #800000;">3</span>:])</p>
<p><span style="color: #808080;"># Returns: ('Honda', 'Ford', 'GMC', 'Fiat')</span></p>
</td>
</tr>
</tbody>
</table>

<p><strong>For example:</strong></p>
<table style="margin: auto; box-shadow: 3px 3px 10px #000;" border="1">
<tbody>
<tr style="background-color: rgba(0,0,0,0.1);">

</table>
